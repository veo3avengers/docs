#### Auron Labs multi-agent orchestration consists of four key agents:

- **Central AI Director** - Orchestrates all production elements
- **Character Design & Animation Agent** - Leverages Veo 3 and Kling 2.1 for voice-synchronized animation
- **Continuity Management Agent** - Ensures scene-to-scene consistency across AI-generated content
- **Quality Assurance Agent** - Monitors output quality and maintains professional standards
